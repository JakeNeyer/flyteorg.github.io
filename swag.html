Grab Flyte Swag

We are grateful to all the Flyte contributors, and we would like to thank you for your support.
Flyte is a community product. Our contributors have been showering us with ideas and contributions all along. We have always wanted to have more such contributions to expand our horizons, and we have been thinking about ways to give back to our community, for all the work that's being done to make Flyte even better.
Here comes our acknowledgement program. Depending on the amount of contributions you have made, you will receive a special badge and swag.
Flyte Pilot

A Flyte Pilot is the one who intensively contributes to Flyte through plugins, blogs, significant features, bug fixes, and advocates for it.

Swag: The North Face® Jacket

Flyte Host/Hostess

A Flyte Host/Hostess is the one who contributes minor features and plugins, content, and bug fixes.

Swag: Voucher or T-Shirt

Flyte Passenger

A Flyte Passenger is the one who contributes minor documentation fixes and bug fixes.

Swag: Mug

If your contribution adds value to Flyte heavily, we will customize your swag and add you as a Flyte contributor!

Note: Every contribution is evaluated thoroughly, and the concerning contributor would be notified.
